Dots & Boxes

How to win:

Players take turns in drawing lines between dots on a grid. The player who completes the most boxes wins.


How to play:

The game is played starting with a rectangular array of dots.
The two players take turns to join two adjacent dots with a horizontal or vertical line. If a player completes the fourth side of a box they initial that box and must draw another line. When all the boxes have been completed the winner is the player who has initialled the most boxes.
